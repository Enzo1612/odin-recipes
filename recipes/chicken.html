Description

Perfectly chewy crust stuffed with cheese, just like a pizzeria chain.

Make your pizza cheese crust with sauce and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Warm Water
  • Brown Sugar
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients together
  2. Grease your pizza pan with some olive oil
  3. Prepare your dough
  4. Cook your pizza in the greased pan
